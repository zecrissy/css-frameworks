Electric Scooters

Author: Crisselda - 06/19/2023

Scooters are reall fun, but an electric scooter is so much more fun! At work, I have to park about five to ten minutes away from my building. Imagine walking in the heat, on the black asphalt four times a day. I said, "no thank you" and I came up with a solution.

My solution was an ELECTRIC SCOOTER! One hurtle was the price tag. Did I really want to pay a lot of money to save me twenty to forty minutes a day? Was it really worth the investemnt? I had to make sure this was going to be worth it.

I spend days looking up different types of electric scooters. Some of the feautures I notices were, pneumatic tires, app sync, front and back suspension, front and rear breaking, and of course speed. To find out if it was worth the expense, check out my second article, Electric Scooter Review.

Electric Scooter Review

Author: Crisselda - 06/19/2023

Excited is definetly the word I would use to describe my emotions. I couldn't wait to get it in the mail and try it out. Of course I had to practice at home fist to get a hang of it and that's what I did. I lost control twice and almost fell off going 15 mph! Luckily I was able to recover and got a sore leg.

The first day at work was nerve wracking. I did not want to fall in front of all my co-workers. However, I stayed calm and rode my scooter to my building. It was amazing! I got to the office in about fifteen seconds! I saved so much time!

I don't regret it at all. It was so worth every penny. It is a bit bummpy on the road, but I don't mind it. With tempatures rising to 107 degrees, it's a life saver. Now I can park far where the trees are to have some shade for my van and still get to work on time.
